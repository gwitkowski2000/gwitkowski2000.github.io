Hello! I'm Grzegorz Witkowski, an enthusiastic embedded systems developer specializing in microcontrollers. With a passion for creating efficient and reliable firmware, I have dedicated the last four years to mastering the art of embedded programming and hardware integration.


My journey began with a fascination for electronics and a knack for problem-solving. This led me to pursue a degree in Automation and Robotics from Kielce University of Technology, where I honed my skills in C/C++, embedded software design, and the usage of various industrial sensors and actuators. Over the years, I've had the pleasure of working on a variety of projects, from developing firmware for IoT devices and home automation to a robot competing in international competitions (together with the team I've even won 1st and 4th places in European Rover Challenge as well as 5th place in University Rover Challenge)


Beyond coding, I am a firm believer in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends. When I'm not immersed in code, you can find me tinkering with electronics, exploring new technologies, or sharing my knowledge.


Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss potential collaborations, interesting projects, or just chat about all things embedded systems!
